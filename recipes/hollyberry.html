Holly Berry Fizz

Description

This holly berry fizz cocktail is vibrant, fragrant, and brings a sophisticated twist to your holiday celebrations with its balance of sweet and floral notes.!

Ingredients

  • 1 fluid ounce pomegranate juice
  • 1 fluid ounce elderflower liqueur, such as St-GermainÂ®
  • 4 fluid ounces chilled prosecco
  • fresh pomegranate arils, for garnish
  • rosemary sprig, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add pomegranate juice and elderflower liqueur to a champagne flute. Top with prosecco, pouring gently to preserve the bubbles.
  2. Drop in a few pomegranate arils and garnish with a sprig of rosemary.
